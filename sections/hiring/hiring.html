JOB VACANTS









Electronics


E.M. Engineer:

Responsible for building the coils around the curved part of the syncrotron for electromagnetic field generation, thus keeping the particles on the track while changing the direction.


Requisites:

Electrical/electronics/industrial engeneering or related knoweledge. Ease with coil building for EM field generation. Knoweledge about wires, AC/DC applications and circuit design.


Linear Acceleration Engineer:

Responsible for designing and building a linear acceleration section using AC, in order to augmentate the inertia of the particle package.


Requisites:

Electrical/electronics/industrial engeneering or related knoweledge. Ease with coil building for EM field generation. Knoweledge about wires, AC/DC applications and circuit design.


Structures and simulation


Material Engineer:

Performs computational simulations and physical testings of the structures in order to determine the propierties of the materials used, stress and possible damages.


Requisites:

Mechanical/industrial/aerospace engeneering student or related knoweledge. Ease and experience in cad and multiphysics software (Solidworks, Ansys, comsol...)


Structural Design Engineer:

Design and build both computational and physical models for synchrotron parts, magnets and other relevant parts of the project.


Requisites:

Mechanical/industrial/aerospace engeneering student or related knoweledge. Ease and experience in cad and multiphysics software (Solidworks, Ansys, comsol...)


Proton generation specialist:

Responsible for investigating, testing and designing different sources of protons, involving chemical, physical or mechanical techniques.


Requisites:

Mechanical/industrial/chemical engeneering student or related knoweledge. Well-balanced science and engineering capabilities. Knoweledge in cad or multiphysics softwares.
